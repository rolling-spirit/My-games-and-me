My favourite hobby

My favourite hobby is gaming. I love to do it. Sometimes I call my friends and they play with me on the ps4 as we battle together. Sometimes I lose but sometimes I win.

Adding to that, gaming is so much fun, really fun. Gaming helps me focus, be observant, build up courage and confidence and also learn how to work as part of a team.

Finally, gaming can be very interesting but you have to learn how to manage your time so you can enjoy your game and not get to obsessed with it.
